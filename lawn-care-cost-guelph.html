How Much Does Lawn Care Cost in Guelph? (2026 Pricing Guide)

June 2026 • 5 min read

Lawn care costs in Guelph vary significantly depending on what you need done and how often. Here's a complete breakdown of what local homeowners are paying in 2026.

Lawn Mowing Prices in Guelph

A typical Guelph residential lawn mowing visit costs $45-$90, with the range depending on lot size:

  • Small lot (under 3,000 sq ft): $45-$60 per visit
  • Medium lot (3,000-6,000 sq ft): $60-$75 per visit
  • Large lot (over 6,000 sq ft): $75-$90+ per visit

Most homeowners on a weekly mowing program spend $130-$250/month during the May-October season. Bi-weekly programs cost roughly 60% of weekly pricing since each visit takes longer when grass is taller.

Fertilizing and Weed Control Costs

A full-season fertilizing and weed control program in Guelph typically runs $250-$450 for a standard residential lot. This usually covers 4-5 applications throughout the season.

Core Aeration Pricing

Core aeration for a typical Guelph home costs $120-$200 as a standalone service. When bundled with overseeding (highly recommended), the combined cost is usually $200-$350.

Overseeding Costs

Overseeding alone runs $80-$180 depending on lot size and seed coverage needed.

Seasonal Lawn Care Programs

A full-service seasonal lawn care program covering mowing, fertilizing, weed control, aeration, and overseeding typically runs $400-$800 per season for a standard Guelph property.

Fall Cleanup

Leaf removal and fall cleanup in Guelph: $150-$350 for a typical residential property, depending on tree coverage and lot size.

What Affects Lawn Care Prices in Guelph?

  • Lot size - the biggest driver of pricing
  • Lawn condition - heavily neglected or weedy lawns take more work initially
  • Accessibility - fenced yards, gates, or slopes add time
  • Frequency - program clients usually get better rates than one-off visits
